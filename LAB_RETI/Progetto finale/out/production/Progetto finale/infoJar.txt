Generare un .jar
- Server
	javac -cp :gson-2.10.jar ServerMain.java
	jar -cvf WordleServer.jar ServerMain.class
- Client
	javac -cp :gson-2.10.jar ClientMain.java
 	jar -cvf WordleClient.jar ClientMain.class

Compilare con un .jar
- Server
	javac -cp :WordleServer.jar:gson-2.10.jar ServerMain.java
- Client
	javac -cp :WordleClient.jar:gson-2.10.jar ClientMain.java
